Hydroponic Growing Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to hydroponic farming! This guide will help you understand the basics of growing plants without soil.

Essential Components:

  • Growing containers
  • Nutrient solution
  • Growing medium (if using)
  • pH testing kit
  • Water pump and air stone

Maintenance Tips:

  • Check pH levels daily
  • Monitor nutrient levels
  • Clean system regularly
  • Watch for pest problems

Common Problems:

  • Nutrient deficiencies
  • pH imbalance
  • Root rot
  • Algae growth
